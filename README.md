# Machine Learning & Computer Vision Curriculum (Self-made)

A curated, structured collection of graduate-level **machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision** courses ‚Äî drawn from world-class university lectures and open educational resources.  
This repository is organized into **phases** and **course folders**, each containing materials, summaries, and notes.

---

## Program Overview

This program is designed as a **self-directed learning pathway** combining:

- Rigorous theoretical foundations (probabilistic modeling, numerical methods)
- Deep learning and applied computer vision
- Modern ML frameworks (PyTorch and JAX)
- Integration of academic and practical perspectives

Each course included here is publicly available, taught by leading researchers in the field, and aligned with contemporary ML research directions.

- **University:** University of T√ºbingen
- **Description:**  
  A deep exploration of probabilistic approaches to machine learning, uncertainty modeling, and inference.  
  Covers topics such as Bayesian reasoning, Gaussian processes, variational inference, and probabilistic numerics.  
  This course provides the theoretical foundation for understanding modern ML beyond deterministic optimization.

### **NML ‚Äì Numerics of Machine Learning**

- **Lecturer:** [Prof. Philipp Hennig](https://uni-tuebingen.de/en/faculties/faculty-of-science/departments/computer-science/department/staff/prof-dr-philipp-hennig/)
- **University:** University of T√ºbingen

- **Lecturer:** [Prof. Andreas Geiger](https://www.cvlibs.net/)
- **University:** University of T√ºbingen
- **Description:**  
  A structured introduction to deep learning, focusing on the mathematical and algorithmic foundations of neural networks.

üìÇ Folder: `./DeepLearning/`

### **CV01 ‚Äì Deep Learning for Computer Vision (CS231n)**

- **Lecturers:**
  - Prof. Fei-Fei Li
  - Prof. Justin Johnson
  - Prof. Serena Yeung
- **University:** Stanford University
- **Description:**  
  One of the most recognized university courses on applying deep learning to visual perception tasks.

  Provides a solid foundation for practical and research-oriented computer vision work.

üìÇ Folder: `./ComputerVisionI/`

- **Lecturer:** [Prof. Andreas Geiger](https://www.cvlibs.net/)
- **University:** University of T√ºbingen

  Integrates deep learning methods with geometric reasoning ‚Äî ideal for understanding 3D perception and robotics applications.

üìÇ Folder: `./PracticalDeepLearning/`
